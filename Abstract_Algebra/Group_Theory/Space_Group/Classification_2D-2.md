# Classification of 2D Space Groups

**Part 2: The 17 Wallpaper Groups**

</font>

<div class="result">

*Theorem*: If $P$ is a *finite subgroup* of $\O(2)$, then

$$
H^2(P, \R^2) = 1 \ (\text{trivial})
$$

</div><br>
