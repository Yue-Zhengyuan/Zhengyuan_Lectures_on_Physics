<div class="remark">

</center>

## Isometries of $\R^2$

<div class="result">

*Theorem*: Any isometry $(a,R) \in \Isom(\R^2)$ is one of the following transformation:

- $\det R = +1$: Translation, rotation
- $\det R = -1$: Reflection, glide reflection

</div><br>

## Isometries of $\R^3$

<div class="result">

*Theorem*: Any isometry $(a,R) \in \Isom(\R^3)$ is one of the following transformation:

- $\det R = +1$: Translation, rotation, screw translation
- $\det R = -1$: Reflection, glide reflection, rotatory reflection

</div><br>
