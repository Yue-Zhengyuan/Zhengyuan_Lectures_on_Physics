## Complex Variables

A general complex bilinear form is

$$
H = z_i^* A_{ij} z_j
$$

<div class="result">

*Theorem*: $H$ is real *if and only if* $A$ is Hermitian ($A^\dagger = A$).

</div>

----
*Proof*: We first calculate $H^*$.

$$
H^* = z_i A_{ij}^* z_j^* = z_j^* (A^\dagger)_{ji} z_i
= z_i^* (A^\dagger)_{ij} z_j \overset{!}{=} H
$$
